Metro Sul is the southbound line that never arrives. A Brazilian melodic techno project by Bruno Gomes, it moves between two times: Chronos — the measured beat — and Kairos — the moment it breaks.

Born in São Paulo's underground, the sound is cinematic, hypnotic, and driven by contrast: light vs. shadow, blue vs. ember, gravity vs. lift. It's techno for late-night transit, for when the city blurs outside the window.
